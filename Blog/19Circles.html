19 Circles Around the Sun

I am officially 19 now!

Today, I am just grateful for all my family and friends who journeyed with me on the path known as life. Without their love, support, and encouragement, I don't know where I would be.

I used to hate celebrating birthdays. For some reason, I would correlate birthday with the idea of "I am one year close to my impending death", which really scares me. However, recently I felt as if I don't fear death as much: I went on the biggest emotional rollercoaster of my life in the past 4 months, and it made me change my outlook on the journey of life.

Feeling all these sorts of emotions in such a short time made me realize how shallow some of my former worries were. Life is something not to be planned and controlled, but something to be experienced. Through living an examined life by exerting effort in something meaningful, I can gain experiences that let me smile in the face of "death" and feel as if I found some sort of meaning and peace. I am glad to say that I have overcome my birthday-phobia.

May my journey be filled with Blessings and Curses!

5-30-24
