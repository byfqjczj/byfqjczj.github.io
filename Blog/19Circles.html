19 Circles Around the Sun

In the beginning, there is only light. Things have only gone downhill since.

The above sentence summarizes the "Genesis" chapter of the bible. Even though god created everything to be holy, mankind succumbed to their temptation and stole a fruit from the sacred tree. Interestingly enough, the fruit grants mankind the ability to differentiate between "good and evil", or in a more general sense, knowledge. However, it is exactly this "knowledge of good and evil" that expelled Adam and Eve from Eden.

This line of thought is better explored in Paradise Lost by Milton, whereas the effects of the "fruit of good and evil" are more apparent, giving mankind feelings of lust, envy, and guilt. It is then elaborated upon that through "gaining knowledge" of these sinful emotions, humans bore the original sin, causing their expulsion from Eden. From a more meta point of view, when human are in their most naive states, they live a sinless life that is devoid of knowledge, but as they mature and grow, they obtain knowledge but become tainted by sin in the process.

Today is also the Genesis of this blog and the genesis of my adult life(somewhat). This summer marked many firsts: the first time in my life working full time, my first summer after college, my first time learning how to drive, and the list goes on. With new experiences comes new knowledge, and a new perception of myself. I still don't how to reconcile with my new knowledge and understanding, especially in a world that feels so strange.

Sometimes I think back to my elementary school days and just reflect on how carefree life was. On those sunny afternoons, I would walk back from school and just have ... fun. There were no ultimate questions to be answered, no struggles to be grappled at. I had no knowledge about "the important things", but that really wasn't needed. What was on my mind most of the time was "what will I get from killing a boss in this MMORPG", and what worried me the most was "what will happen if my favorite character died, and I lost all the cool items". Looking back, it felt so silly that I would worry about such things, but I feel so jealous and angry that I am physically and emotionally unable to worry about such things anymore.

To the end of my childhood, and the genesis of my adulthood.
